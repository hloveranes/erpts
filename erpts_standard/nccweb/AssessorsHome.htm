Welcome to the e-RPTS Assessor’s Module.
From the Assessor’s Module you can:

Search for any information in one of the following documents:
  • Owner’s Declaration (OD)
  • Assessor’s Field Sheet / Field Appraisal and Assessment Sheet (AFS / FAAS)
  • Tax Declaration (TD)
  • Real Property Tax Order of Payment (RPTOP)
You can also encode new real property information. To do so, begin with the Owner’s Declaration screen and go through the appropriate steps:
  • Encode Property Information (in the OD)
  • Encode or Select Owner Information (in the OD)
  • Encode Real Property Information (in the AFS / FAAS)
  • Encode Tax-related Information (in the TD)
  • Generate RPTOP
If you encounter any problems, please consult your LGU’s System Administrator or the appropriate NCC personnel listed in the Assessor’s Module User’s Manual provided by the NCC.
