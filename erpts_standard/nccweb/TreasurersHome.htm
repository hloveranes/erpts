Welcome to the e-RPTS Treasurer's Module
 
The Treasurer's Module provides functionality for the treasurer to facilitate the various aspects of tax collection after the assessment of real property and generation of the Tax Declarations coming from the Assessor's Office. The following are the main activities provided in the main navigation
  • Calculate Taxes - First selecting the previously generated RPTOP, a calculation of the Tax Dues is performed and provided prior to printing the RPTOP. The RPTOP should then be sent to the taxpayer to notify them of the taxes due to the LGU stemming from their ownership of the propery. The RPTOP and its dues are subsequently updated in the database.
  • Pay Taxes - As taxpayers present the RPTOP, the information is encode into the system along with their payment, and a Real Property Tax Receipt is issued per TD payment.
  • Penalties - for inquiries on pending tax dues, penalties can be calculated to present the cumulative arrears of the taxpayer for the particular TD.
  • Statement of Account (SOA) - given a particular TD, the complete reckoning of past delinquencies and arrears as due to the LGU and recorded in the system can be calculated and presented. The SOA can be printed for notification and reference to taxpayer and government officials concerned.

There are other aspects of collection that might be of periodic if not temporal interest to the treasurer.

Batch encoding. In the initial setup of the RPTS system, it is important to encode as much of the information collected and recorded on paper into the electronic database for ease in retrieval as well as completeness of the data provided by the eRPTS.

Report Generation. A periodic roll of the SOA and other reports can be provided by the system and will be most useful after a successful batch encoding of existing information.
